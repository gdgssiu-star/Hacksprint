What is HackSprint?

About The Event

HackSprint 2026 is a premier 12-hour hybrid hackathon organized by Google Developer Group On Campus (GDGOC) at Swarrnim Startup and Innovation University. This event brings together students, developers, and innovators to create impactful solutions leveraging Google technologies and modern development tools. Whether you join us on-campus in Gandhinagar or participate online, you'll experience a day of intensive learning, collaboration, and innovation.

üíª

Hybrid Format

Participate on-campus at our state-of-the-art Gandhinagar facility for the complete hackathon experience, or join online from anywhere in India. Both modes offer equal opportunities for mentorship, collaboration, and winning prizes.

üéØ

Open Innovation

Build solutions across any domain - healthcare, education, sustainability, fintech, or social impact. Leverage Google technologies including Firebase, Google Cloud Platform, TensorFlow, Flutter, and more to create innovative applications that solve real-world problems.

Competitive Rewards

Compete for a total prize pool of ‚Çπ35,000 across multiple categories. Winners receive cash prizes, certificates of achievement, exclusive swag, and additional rewards from Google Developer Groups. All participants receive certificates and learning resources.

Team Size: 1-5 members per team
